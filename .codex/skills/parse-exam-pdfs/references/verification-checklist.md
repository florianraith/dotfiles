# Verification Checklist

- Every source exam has exactly one output file.
- Every output identifies its exam and solution source.
- Exercise order matches the exam.
- Task descriptions come from the exam, not the solution.
- Solutions occur immediately after their task.
- `##` is used for tasks and solutions; `###` is used for subtasks.
- Task and solution counts match the source.
- Page-spanning descriptions are complete.
- Tables, code, formulas, diagrams, and handwritten answers were visually checked.
- Rendered visual assets are sharp and linked from the relevant task.
- All relative image links exist.
- OCR substitutions and ligature damage were corrected.
- Running headers, footers, names, page numbers, watermarks, and answer-space noise were removed.
- Repeated bilingual sections were not duplicated.
- No `�` characters or placeholder text remain.
